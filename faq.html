Services & Process

07 questions
We offer five core digital services: Custom Website Development, AI Chatbot Solutions, Business Automation Systems, Professional Business Email Setup, and Digital Branding & Marketing Assets. Each service is available independently or as part of a combined package — and when used together, the results compound significantly. Visit our Services page for full details on each.
Timelines vary by scope and service. As a guide: website projects typically take 2–4 weeks; AI chatbot deployments take 1–3 weeks; automation builds take 1–2 weeks; email setup is usually completed within 2–3 business days; and branding packages take 2–3 weeks. We provide a clear, confirmed timeline before any work begins — no surprises, no scope creep.
Yes — and it's actually our preferred way to work. When website, AI, automation, branding, and email are built together under one roof, everything integrates seamlessly. There's no coordination overhead, no conflicting design decisions, and no gaps. The end result is a cohesive, high-performing digital operation rather than a collection of disconnected tools.
Every project follows four stages: Discovery (we learn your goals, audience, and constraints); Design & Architecture (we plan and present the approach for your approval); Build & Refine (we develop with regular check-ins and revision rounds); and Launch & Handover (we go live, verify everything, and transfer ownership to you). You'll always know exactly where your project stands.
We build revision rounds into every project scope. For most engagements, two structured revision rounds are included — enough to refine the work to exactly where it needs to be. Our discovery process is thorough precisely to minimise the need for major revisions. Any significant changes beyond the agreed scope are discussed openly before proceeding.
All projects include a post-launch support window to address any issues that arise after going live. Beyond that, we offer ongoing retainer arrangements covering maintenance, updates, performance optimisation, and continued development. Many of our clients stay with us on a monthly basis — we become the dedicated digital team working in the background as they grow.
No — our services are industry-agnostic. We've worked with businesses across e-commerce, professional services, hospitality, health, education, real estate, and more. What matters is not your industry but your ambition. If you're serious about modernising and growing your business, we're the right partner.

Technical Questions

06 questions
We select the technology stack best suited to your specific requirements. For most projects, we build with modern HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for complete custom control, or use frameworks like React or Next.js for dynamic applications. For clients who need a CMS, we work with WordPress (headless or traditional), Webflow, or Sanity depending on your team's capabilities and future content needs. We never impose a stack — we recommend what's right for you.
Yes. Technical SEO foundations are built into every website we deliver — clean semantic HTML, fast page load times, proper meta structures, sitemap generation, schema markup, and mobile optimisation. We ensure search engines can crawl and index your site correctly from day one. We also offer ongoing SEO services for clients who want to build organic visibility over time.
We work across the leading automation platforms including Make (Integromat), Zapier, n8n, and custom API integrations. We connect these to your existing tools — whether that's a CRM, email marketing platform, Google Workspace, Slack, WhatsApp, payment processors, or any other system in your stack. If it has an API, we can most likely automate it.
Our AI chatbots are built to integrate with any existing website via a lightweight embed code — no rebuild required. The bot can be deployed as a chat widget, inline component, or full-page interface depending on your needs. It connects to your CRM, calendar, email, or support tools to route conversations and capture data in real time. Multi-channel deployment (including WhatsApp Business) is also available.
Absolutely. If your project includes a CMS, we configure it to be genuinely user-friendly — not the overly technical setup that most agencies hand over. We document everything and provide a handover session to walk you through updating content, adding pages, and managing your site confidently. You'll never be held hostage to our availability for basic updates.
We provide full guidance on hosting and domain configuration and can handle the setup entirely on your behalf. We recommend platforms based on your site's needs — typically Vercel, Netlify, or managed WordPress hosting for performance and reliability. All hosting accounts are set up in your name so you retain full ownership and control of your digital assets at all times.

Getting Started

05 questions
Getting started is simple. Reach out via email at hello@geobrif.com with a brief overview of your business and what you're looking to achieve. We'll respond within 24 hours to arrange a focused discovery call — no lengthy forms, no sales pressure. From there, we'll prepare a tailored proposal outlining scope, timeline, and investment within 48 hours.
Yes — and we enjoy it. Starting from scratch means there are no bad decisions to undo and no legacy systems to work around. We can take you from zero to a fully operational digital business: domain registration, professional email, website, branding, and AI systems — all set up properly from the beginning. Many of our clients start with us at the very foundation stage.
Absolutely. GeoBrif Digital is a fully remote, internationally-oriented studio. We have clients across multiple countries and time zones. Our entire workflow — from discovery to delivery — is designed to run seamlessly online. Communication happens via email, video call, and shared project spaces. Distance has never been a barrier to producing exceptional work.
You don't need to come with everything figured out — that's our job. But it helps to have a rough sense of: what your business does and who your customers are; what specific problem you're trying to solve; any existing digital assets (website, social media, branding); and your rough timeline expectations. The more context you can share upfront, the more precise and actionable our proposal will be.
We operate on a split-payment model for most projects: a deposit is required to begin, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. For larger or longer engagements, milestone-based payment schedules are available. All payment details and terms are clearly outlined in your project proposal before any commitment is made — there are no hidden fees or unexpected costs.
